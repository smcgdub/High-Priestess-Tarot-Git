I offer personal, private Tarot card readings for guidance, direction, advice and focus. You can book a face to face reading in London or receive a reading via phone or video call.

Why get a tarot card reading?

Subconsciously we are all moving ourselves along a certain path. Every day, hundreds of times a day, we make little decisions that alter or lead us in a direction. But we're not always consciously aware of where we're headed, nor do we clearly see the other paths or hidden influences of our decisions. A Tarot reading helps us to connect with a wider consciousness, to illuminate the path we are currently on, show us other possible paths, and explore the likely outcomes of our decisions.

What to expect from your reading

Whether you've had a tarot reading before or not, you may have lots of questions. The important thing to remember is that there are no ‘good' or ‘bad' cards. Each and every tarot card drawn for your personal reading will have many different layers of meaning. The intuition of your tarot card reader is required along with knowledge of the individual cards in order to weave together the message that is meant for you.

It is up to you how much background information you want to give to your question. The cards aren’t always specific about people or timing, so sometimes it’s easier to share some context to make sure you get the most out of your session, but it’s up to you. You can also ask questions during the reading to find out more about something that is coming up.

A tarot card reading is not 'fortune telling' in the traditional sense because your path is not set - you are ultimately the master of your destiny. But the tarot reading will have you to demystify it by revealing insight and hidden influences to help you make decisions, and advice for the best outcome based on the path you are on.
