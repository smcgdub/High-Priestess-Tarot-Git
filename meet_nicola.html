    About The High Priestess Tarot

    Hello, I'm Nicola, from the moment I picked up my first pack of tarot cards 20 years ago, I felt a connection to something magical.

    Most often I give tarot readings on my houseboat in London. The world of the traditional canal boats and the calming influence of the water makes this my favourite place for giving tarot readings.

    After deciding to read Tarot professionally, I completed a Tarot foundation course with Treadwell's in London, and I began training in Spiritual Psychotherapy and Holistic Healing. This has taught me invaluable skills on how to read Tarot on a deeper level, how to develop intuitively, and ethical practise and use of the cards.

    I have given tarot card readings to people from all walks of life, including the Sidemen on YouTube, to people at parties, at festivals, via email, at clubs and at cafes. As a reader, I have been described as being warm, open and non-judgmental, with spot-on accuracy. I have read for people who are totally cynical, very spiritual, and completely agnostic. The cards always come through, giving insightful and practical advice.

    I offer personal, private Tarot card readings for guidance, direction, advice and focus. You can book a face to face reading in London or receive a reading via email or Skype.

    Why get a tarot card reading?

    Subconsciously we are all moving ourselves along a certain path. Every day, hundreds of times a day, we make little decisions that alter or lead us in a direction. But we're not always consciously aware of where we're headed, nor do we clearly see the other paths or hidden influences of our decisions. A Tarot reading helps us to connect with our subconscious, to illuminate the path we are currently on, show us other possible paths, and explore the likely outcomes of our decisions.

    What to expect from your reading

    Whether you've had a tarot reading before or not, you may have lots of questions. The important thing to remember is that there are no 'good' or 'bad' cards. Each and every tarot card drawn for your personal reading will have many different layers of meaning. The intuition of your tarot card reader is required along with knowledge of the individual cards in order to weave together the message that is meant for you.

    A tarot card reading is not 'fortune telling' in the traditional sense because your path is not set - you are ultimately the master of your destiny. But the tarot reading will have you to demystify it by revealing insight and hidden influences to help you make decisions, and advice for the best outcome based on the path you are on.
